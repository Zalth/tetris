About the game

Rotating the shapes clockwise or counterclockwise:

There are 7 different possible shapes that can be made using four squares. Most of these shapes can be rotated in up to four different orientations using the 'D' and 'F' keys on the keyboard or the curved arrow buttons on the side of the game grid area.

Moving the shapes left or right:

The shapes will appear at the top of the game grid area and fall. You can move the shape left or right using the arrow keys on the keyboard or the arrow bottons on the side of the game grid area.

Goal of the game:

Your goal is the completely fill one or more lines across the game grid area with the falling shapes. As a line is completed, it will disappear and all filled grids above that line will drop down. You will get more points by completing more rows with a single dropped shape.

Leveling up:

Your level will increase every 10 lines that are completed. As your level increases the shapes will fall more quickly and your score for completed lines will increase.

Losing the game:

The game will end if a newly generated shape appears on top of a previous shape.
